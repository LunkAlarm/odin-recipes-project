Lemon and Lavender Gin Fizz

  • One and a half ounces citrusy-herbal gin
  • One tablespoon fresh lemon juice (one lemon)
  • One tablespoon simple syrup
  • One dash Scrappy's Lavender Bitters
  • Ice
  • Two tablespoons club soda
  • Two dashes Peychaud's bitters
  • Garnish with lemon wheels,dried juniper berries and fresh rose petals
  • Enjoy
